# Code of Conduct

We are committed to creating a harassment-free, inclusive, and respectful environment for all participants.

## Acceptable Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Engage in constructive feedback and collaboration.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, offensive language, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination or hate speech.
